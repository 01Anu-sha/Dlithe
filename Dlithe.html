Paris

🌟 Famous Places to Visit in Paris

1️⃣ Eiffel Tower 🗼

The most famous landmark of Paris and a global symbol of France.

2️⃣ Louvre Museum 🎨

The world’s largest art museum, home to the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.

3️⃣ Notre-Dame Cathedral ⛪

A stunning Gothic cathedral with amazing architecture.

4️⃣ Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe 🇫🇷

Avenue des Champs-Élysées is the most famous shopping street in Paris.

5️⃣ Sacré-Cœur & Montmartre 🎭

The Sacré-Cœur Basilica, located on Montmartre hill, offers the best city view.

6️⃣ Seine River Cruise 🚢

A boat cruise on the Seine River offers the best views of Parisian landmarks!

7️⃣ Palace of Versailles 🏰

A grand royal palace with stunning gardens and the famous Hall of Mirrors.

8️⃣ Disneyland Paris 🎢

A magical theme park perfect for families and Disney fans.

💡 Best Time to Visit Paris

✅ April - June & September - November

✅ December for Christmas markets and beautiful lights!

Bali

🌟 Famous Places to Visit in Bali

1️⃣ Uluwatu Temple 🛕

A clifftop temple offering breathtaking ocean views.

2️⃣ Tanah Lot Temple 🌊

A beautiful sea temple built on a rock formation.

3️⃣ Tegallalang Rice Terraces 🌾

One of Bali’s most iconic landscapes. A great spot for photography and swing rides.

4️⃣ Ubud Monkey Forest 🐒

A lush jungle with ancient temples and hundreds of mountain.

5️⃣ Mount Batur 🌋

A volcanic mountain where you can hike to see a sunrise.

6️⃣ Seminyak Beach 🏖️

A hotspot for luxury resorts, beach clubs, and surfing.

7️⃣ Nusa Penida Island 🏝️

Known for Kelingking Beach, a T-Rex-shaped cliff.

8️⃣ Bali Swing 🌄

A famous giant swing experience over rice fields.📸

💡 Best Time to Visit Bali

✅ April - October

✅ Avoid the rainy season (November - March) if you want clear skies.
